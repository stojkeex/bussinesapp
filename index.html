Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vaulty AI really free?

Yes! Vaulty offers a free Basic plan that is great for exploring basic ideas and features. For more advanced tools and unlimited use, premium plans are available.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Of course. You can easily cancel your subscription with one click in your account settings. You will retain access to premium features until the end of the paid period.

Why do I need to sign up?

Registration is required so we can securely save all your projects, business plans, and settings. This allows you to access your work anytime and from any device.

How reliable is the data provided by the AI?

Our AI is trained on a vast amount of data to provide you with the most accurate and relevant information possible. However, with rapidly changing data (like prices or specific regulations), it can sometimes be mistaken. Therefore, we recommend always double-checking key data yourself.
